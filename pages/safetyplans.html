You can break the cycle. The sooner you leave, the sooner you can start to heal.

The checklist below will help you plan.

You may not have the ability to wait until you have things together. Make sure that you are safe. However, if you do have the time, the following lists will help you to prepare.

  • Passports
  • Driver's license or other official ID
  • Birth certificate(s)
  • Cash
  • Essential medications and prescriptions
  • Cellphone, preferably a 'burner phone' that is not connected to you in any traceable way
  • Safety deposit key or other banking information
  • Grab your coat
  • Wear comfortable shoes
  • Find a safe exit route from your house
  • Memorize phone numbers of friends, relatives, and especially shelters and other resources
  • Memorize the addresses of several local shelters and a police station
  • Make sure everything that might indicate your location, such as written-down passwords or maps, are not visible.
  • If you work, take a different route to your job site.
